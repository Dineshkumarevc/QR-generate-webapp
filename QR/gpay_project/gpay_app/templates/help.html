Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the Help section. Below are some frequently asked questions to help you understand how to use the GPay Clone app.

1. How do I create a payment?

To create a payment, click on the 'Create Payment' button on the homepage. Enter the necessary details and follow the instructions.

2. How do I link my bank account?

To link your bank account, click on the 'Bank Details' button. Enter your bank information in the form and save it for future transactions.

3. How can I view my past transactions?

To view your past transactions, go to the 'Settings' section and select 'Transaction History' to view a list of all previous transactions.

4. How do I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' option on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

5. Who do I contact for support?

If you need assistance, you can contact us via the 'Contact' section of the app, where you can send us a message or get in touch through email or phone.
